Negative Cycle Arbitrage Graph

This graph shows each cryptocurrency at a number of exchanges as vertices. The graph's edges can be thought of as the exchange rates between these currencies. We use a modified Bellman Ford algorithm to look for negative cycles in the graph, and these negative cycles are seen as the vertices and edges in the center of the graph.
